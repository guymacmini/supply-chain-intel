### Moat Analysis

**Durable Moats:**
- **IBM's Ecosystem Moat**: Qiskit has become the de facto standard for quantum software development with 500,000+ users. This network effect creates sticky relationships with enterprise developers and academics, making IBM a likely platform winner regardless of hardware evolution.
- **Quantinuum's Quality Moat**: Consistently demonstrates highest quantum volume and lowest error rates. For enterprise customers where reliability matters, this quality differentiation justifies premium pricing.

**Eroding Moats:**
- **IonQ's Technology Lead**: While IonQ pioneered commercial trapped-ion systems, Quantinuum has caught up on fidelity metrics. IonQ's moat depends on continued innovation and commercial execution.

- **First-Mover Advantages Generally**: The technology is evolving so rapidly that early leads can be overcome. Customer relationships and ecosystem lock-in matter more than pure technical specifications.
